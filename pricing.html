Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SESMine pricing

Is the free plan really free forever?
Yes. The Free plan includes the Economics Hub, Cost Calculator and 5 AI Predictor runs per month — permanently, with no credit card required.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?
Absolutely. Cancel anytime from your account settings. You keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period, then revert to Free.
What does the 14-day Pro trial include?
Full access to all 6 hubs, unlimited AI Predictor runs, Equipment Database, ESG reporting and PDF exports — everything in Pro, free for 14 days.
Do you offer discounts for students or universities?
Yes — 80% discount for verified students and academic institutions. Contact us at hello@sesmine.com with your university email for access.
How accurate is the AI CAPEX Predictor?
±3.2% mean absolute error on our validation set of 480 held-out projects. Accuracy varies by study stage — DFS inputs yield tighter ranges than scoping studies.
Is my data secure and private?
All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never share or sell your project data. Enterprise customers can request a full security review and DPA.
